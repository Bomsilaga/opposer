Payment & Access — Step by Step
How Payment Works

Complete, honest breakdown. No surprises.

1
Click Subscribe above. You'll be taken to a Gumroad checkout page. Gumroad is a trusted global payment platform — they handle your card details securely. We never see your card number.
2
Enter your email and pay. Choose the plan that fits. Pay with any major card or PayPal. Gumroad shows the price in your local currency automatically.
3
Gumroad emails you a receipt instantly. The email contains your access link or licence key. Click it — you'll be redirected back here with your tier unlocked automatically using a token in the URL.
4
Your session is activated. The app reads the token, verifies it against our backend (Supabase), and upgrades your account. You'll see your tier badge change from FREE TRIAL to your plan name immediately.
5
Renewals are automatic. Gumroad charges your card on the same day each month. You'll get an email reminder 3 days before. If payment fails, Gumroad retries 3 times over 5 days then cancels — you get an email at each stage.
6
To cancel: Log in to boboma.gumroad.com → Library → find your Opposer subscription → Cancel. Or email us and we'll do it within 24 hours. No questions asked. Your access stays active until the end of the current billing period.
7
Students: The Student plan requires a .edu, .ac.uk, .edu.au or equivalent institutional email. If you don't have one, email us proof of enrolment and we'll manually activate you at the student rate.
8
Refunds: If you're not satisfied within 7 days of your first payment, email us for a full refund. No questions asked.
